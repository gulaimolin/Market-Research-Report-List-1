<p><p>The super resolution microscope system market includes various types of advanced imaging technologies. Stimulation Emission Depletion (STED) microscopy uses laser beams to reduce the fluorescence of molecules surrounding the targeted area, enhancing resolution. Structured Illumination Microscopy (SIM) and Spatially Structured Illumination Microscopy (SSIM) utilize patterned lighting to create high-resolution images. STORM (Stochastic Optical Reconstruction Microscopy) enables visualization of subcellular structures by activating and deactivating individual fluorophores. FPALM (Fluorescence Photoactivation Localization Microscopy) and PALM (Photoactivated Localization Microscopy) use photoactivation to precisely locate fluorescent molecules, providing detailed images of cellular components.</p></p>
