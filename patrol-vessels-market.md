Many countries are investing in the procurement of patrol vessels equipped with advanced sensors, communication systems, and unmanned aerial vehicles (UAVs) for efficient surveillance and interdiction.</p><p>Furthermore, the growing popularity of multi-mission patrol vessels capable of conducting a wide range of operations, such as search and rescue, fisheries protection, and environmental monitoring, is fueling market growth.</p><p>The future outlook for the patrol vessels market is expected to be promising.
