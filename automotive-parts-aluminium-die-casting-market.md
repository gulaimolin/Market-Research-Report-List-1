<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The automotive parts aluminium die casting market is composed of four main types: pressure die casting, vacuum die casting, squeeze die casting, and semi-solid die casting. Pressure die casting involves injecting molten aluminum into a die cavity under high pressure. Vacuum die casting is a similar process, but with a vacuum applied to remove air from the mold cavity. Squeeze die casting utilizes a lower casting pressure and a longer filling time. Finally, semi-solid die casting involves using partially solidified aluminum in the casting process.</p></p>
