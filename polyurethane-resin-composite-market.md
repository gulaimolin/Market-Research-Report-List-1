<p><p>Polyurethane resin composite refers to a material made by combining polyurethane resin with a reinforcing fiber. There are different types of market for polyurethane resin composite, including pultrusion, winding, and perfusion. In pultrusion, the composite material is pulled through a heated die to form a continuous profile. Winding involves wrapping continuous fiber reinforcement around a mandrel or core to create a hollow structure. Perfusion, on the other hand, involves impregnating fiber reinforcement with resin and applying pressure to distribute the resin evenly. These techniques are used to manufacture a range of products in industries such as construction, automotive, and aerospace.</p></p>
