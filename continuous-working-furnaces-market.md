<p><p>Continuous working furnaces are used for various heat treatment processes in industries. Pusher-type continuous furnaces use a system of pusher dogs to move the material through the furnace. These are suitable for medium to heavy loads. Walking-beam continuous furnaces have a walking beam mechanism to transport the material. These are suitable for lighter loads and achieve better heat uniformity. Roller type heat treatment furnaces use a roller conveyor to move the material. Other types of continuous working furnaces include mesh belt and rotary retort furnaces. These furnaces are used in different industries for processes such as annealing, tempering, and quenching.</p></p>
