<p><p>Latex paints refer to water-based paints that contain latex or synthetic polymers as binders. The market for latex paints is diversified into various types, including interior latex paint, universal latex paint, anti-fouling latex paint, and antibacterial latex paint. </p><p>Interior latex paint is specifically designed for use on interior walls and provides a smooth, durable finish. Universal latex paint is versatile and suitable for both interior and exterior applications. Anti-fouling latex paint is formulated to prevent the growth of organisms on surfaces exposed to water, such as boats and underwater structures. Antibacterial latex paint contains additives that inhibit the growth of bacteria on painted surfaces, making it suitable for healthcare facilities, kitchens, and other hygienic environments.</p></p>
