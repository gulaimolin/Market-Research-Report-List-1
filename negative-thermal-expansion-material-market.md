<p><p>Negative thermal expansion (NTE) materials are a class of materials that exhibit the unique property of contracting, rather than expanding, when subjected to an increase in temperature. These materials find significant applications in industries such as aerospace, electronics, healthcare, and energy storage.</p><p>The future outlook for the negative thermal expansion material market is promising. The market is expected to experience substantial growth in the coming years, primarily driven by increasing demand in the automotive and aerospace sectors.
