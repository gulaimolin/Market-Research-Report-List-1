<p><p>The glass-lined evaporator market comprises two prominent types, namely L-Rotor and P-Rotor. L-Rotor design includes a long shaft with blades, creating a fluid flow that aids evaporation while preventing crystallization. P-Rotor design involves a powerful agitator that continuously scrapes the heat exchange surfaces, ensuring effective evaporation and eliminating scaling. Both types provide efficient heat transfer, prevent product degradation, and maintain uniformity throughout the evaporation process. The choice between L-Rotor and P-Rotor depends on specific application requirements and preferences for enhanced productivity and reduced maintenance.</p></p>
