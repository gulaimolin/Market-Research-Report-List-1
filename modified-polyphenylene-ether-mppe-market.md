<p><p>Modified Polyphenylene Ether (MPPE) is a thermoplastic resin that is commonly used in various applications. It finds its market in different types such as PPE/PS, PPE/PA, PPE/PP, and Others. PPE/PS refers to the combination of MPPE with polystyrene, offering enhanced toughness and dimensional stability. PPE/PA combines MPPE with polyamide, providing good mechanical properties and resistance to chemicals. PPE/PP signifies the mixture of MPPE with polypropylene, resulting in improved impact strength and thermal resistance. Lastly, the Others category represents other variations of MPPE blends with different polymers to meet specific requirements in various industries.</p></p>
