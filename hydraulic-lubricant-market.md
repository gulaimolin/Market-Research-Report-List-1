<p><p>The hydraulic lubricant market is divided into two main types: mineral lubricants and synthetic lubricants. Mineral lubricants are derived from refined crude oil and are known for their cost-effectiveness and good thermal stability. They are commonly used in applications where high temperatures and pressures are not present. On the other hand, synthetic lubricants are artificially produced and offer superior performance even in extreme conditions. They have higher thermal stability, improved oxidation resistance, and better viscosity index, making them suitable for applications with high temperatures and pressures.</p></p>
