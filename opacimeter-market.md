<p><p>Opacimeter is a device used to measure the opacity or degree of light transmission through a medium. There are two main types of opacimeters: filter paper opacimeters and transmissive opacimeters. </p><p>Filter paper opacimeters use a special type of filter paper with controlled light transmission to determine the opacity of a substance. This type of opacimeter is commonly used in industries such as automotive, where it is used to measure the opacity of exhaust gases to monitor pollution levels. </p><p>Transmissive opacimeters, on the other hand, utilize a light source and a detector to measure the light transmission through a medium. This type of opacimeter is often used in scientific research, environmental monitoring, and quality control to measure the opacity of liquids, gases, or solid materials.</p></p>
