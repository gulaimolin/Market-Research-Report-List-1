<p><p>Commercial car tachographs refer to devices used to record driving time and other related data for commercial vehicles. The market for such tachographs can be categorized into two types: integrated and portable. Integrated tachographs are permanently installed in the vehicle's dashboard and are typically used in larger fleet operations. On the other hand, portable tachographs are smaller and can be easily moved between vehicles, making them suitable for smaller businesses or individual drivers. Both types serve the purpose of monitoring and ensuring compliance with regulations regarding driving and rest times.</p></p>
