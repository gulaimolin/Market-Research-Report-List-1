<p><p>Synthetic compressor oil is classified into three types: PAO (polyalphaolefin), PAG (polyalkylene glycol), and Esters. PAO is a common type of synthetic oil that offers excellent oxidation resistance and thermal stability, making it suitable for high-temperature applications. PAG oil is known for its superior lubricating properties and compatibility with various compressor types. Esters, on the other hand, provide excellent lubricity and are often used in applications where resistance to chemical breakdown is crucial. These different types offer various advantages, and their usage depends on the specific needs of the compressor system.</p></p>
