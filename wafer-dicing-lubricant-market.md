There are four main types of lubricants used in this market. Polyoxyethylene glycol base lubricants are known for their high thermal stability and low viscosity. Ethylene glycol base lubricants offer excellent cooling properties and good lubricity. Polyalkylene glycol base lubricants have high viscosity and are preferred for heavy-duty applications. Lastly, other lubricants include additives like surfactants or antioxidants to enhance performance and protect the semiconductor wafers.</p></p>
